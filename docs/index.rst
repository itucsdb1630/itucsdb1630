**Light Movie DataBase**

We love watching movies, make lists for different emotions and times.

Current movie database sites became very complex and interaction between users are minimum.
Our idea is to create social network on movies for movie lovers like us.
Share ideas, follow professionals, reviewers and friends, review movies, make your playlists.
Get notification when new movies added by your favorite director or in your lovely categories.

We are Movie Discussion Network.
